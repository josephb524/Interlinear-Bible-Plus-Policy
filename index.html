Privacy Policy for Interlinear Bible Plus Last Updated: Nov 01 2025 Interlinear Bible ("we," "our," or "the app") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app. 1. Information We Collect The app is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information: A. Subscription Information (via Apple) When you subscribe, Apple processes payment and shares transaction/subscription status with the app via StoreKit. This includes subscription status, expiration dates, and renewal information. We do not process, store, or have access to your payment details; Apple handles all transactions. B. Local App Data (Stored on Your Device Only) User preferences: color scheme, font style, font size, selected Bible version. Personal content: verse highlights, bookmarks, and optional notes you create. Usage metrics: app launches, chapters viewed, bookmarks added (used only for in-app prompts like review requests). Interaction data: whether you’ve dismissed review or support prompts, and when. 2. How We Use Your Information Process and manage subscriptions through Apple’s StoreKit. Provide and improve app features and functionality. Personalize your experience based on preferences. Show review/support prompts based on engagement. Comply with legal obligations. 3. Data Storage and Security All data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. No personal data is transmitted to our servers. Subscription data is handled by Apple through StoreKit. We do not have access to your Apple ID, email, or payment information. 4. Data Sharing and Third Parties We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Apple handles subscription transactions; their policies apply. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. 5. Your Rights and Choices Delete data: Delete the app to remove all local data. Manage subscriptions: Use your iOS device settings to manage, cancel, or restore subscriptions. Preferences: Adjust app settings at any time. 6. Children’s Privacy The app is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, contact us. 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance. 8. Contact Us For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through: App Store contact options The support features in the app 9. Data Retention Local data (preferences, highlights, bookmarks) remains on your device until you delete the app or manually remove it. Subscription status is maintained by Apple and the app only accesses current status. 10. No Personal Identification Required The app does not require accounts, email, names, or other personal identifiers. All functionality is available without providing personal information beyond what Apple shares via StoreKit for subscription management. By using Interlinear Bible Plus, you acknowledge you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
